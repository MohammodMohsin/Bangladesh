THE SANGU





The origin of the Sangu river is in the Arakan Mountain. Originating from the boundary of Myanmar and Bangladesh and flowing

through Bangladesh and Chittagong. The river falls at the south of the mouth of Karnaphuli into the Bay of Bengal.
